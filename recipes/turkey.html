Chef Ben's Recipes for Dogs

Description

Ben's Turkey Temptation is a recipe that brings back fond memories for Chef Ben. As a young pup, his mother used to make him this delicious meal, using only the freshest and most succulent turkey. She would slow-cook the turkey to perfection, infusing it with herbs and spices to create a mouth-watering aroma and a taste that was out of this world. As he grew older, Chef Ben inherited his mother's recipe and has been perfecting it ever since. Now, he shares this treasured recipe with other dogs, so they can treat themselves to the same delicious meal that brought him so much joy as a pup.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Ground turkey
  • Sweet potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Zucchini squash
  • Broccoli
  • All-Natural chicken stock
  • Macaroni noodles

Steps

  1. Cook turkey: In a large Dutch oven, heat oil over medium heat. Add ground turkey, begin to break it down, and cook for 8-10 minutes, or until mostly cooked through.

  2. Chop veggies: With a food processor, finely chop sweet potatoes, carrots, zucchini and broccoli.

  3. Add vegetables and stock: Add in the chopped vegetables and chicken stock (or water). Cook on a strong simmer for 7-8 minutes, or until veggies are tender and cooked through.

  4. Stir in pasta: Mix in your cooked pasta noodles to the turkey vegetable mixture.
