Ben's Salmon Delight

Description

Ben's Salmon Delight is a homemade recipe created by the culinary-minded chef Ben. This recipe features fresh and succulent salmon as the main ingredient, providing a delicious and nutritious meal for any dog. Ben carefully selects the best quality ingredients to create a meal that not only tastes great but also provides essential nutrients to support a healthy diet. With step by step instructions and easy to follow recipe, even a human can make this meal for their furry friend. Whether you're looking for a special treat or a regular meal option, Ben's Salmon Delight is sure to please even the pickiest of eaters.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of fish fillets
  • 1 or 2 cans of pink salmon
  • 3 whole eggs (raw or cooked)
  • 3 cups of diced vegetables
  • 1 cup of cooked rice (white or brown)

Steps

  1. Dice up the fish fillets or grind them, then cook it.

  2. Heat your vegetables with water until they boil. Simmer for about 5 minutes. Drain.

  3. Combine fish, canned salmon, vegetables, and rice in a large bowl.

  4. Cool before serving.

  5. Store in a sealed container for up to three days, or freeze.
